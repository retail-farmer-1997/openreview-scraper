# Core Beliefs

Owner: retail-farmer-1997
Last Reviewed: 2026-03-18
Status: Active

## Agent-First Engineering
- Humans define outcomes, constraints, and priorities.
- Agents execute implementation, testing, and iteration.
- Repository-local artifacts are the only durable memory.

## Legibility Over Cleverness
- Optimize for discoverability and predictable structure.
- Keep business rules explicit and close to their boundaries.
- Prefer boring, inspectable patterns over opaque abstractions.

## Mechanical Enforcement
- Convert repeated review comments into checks and tests.
- Use scripts and CI to enforce invariants.
- Keep guidance short at the entry point and deep in linked docs.
